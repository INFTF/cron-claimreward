**ClaimReward Automation** is a simple hook that automatically claims your Xahau monthly reward on schedule — no reminders, no manual transactions, no missed payouts.

Once installed, your account quietly takes care of itself.

## What It Does
- Automatically submits **ClaimReward** every month  
- Uses a safe schedule: **30 days + 3 hours + 13 minutes**  
- Runs up to **256 times** without needing reinstallation  
- Requires no maintenance or interaction after setup  
- Only performs ClaimReward — nothing else

## Who It's For
- Anyone who forgets to claim manually  
- Low-maintenance or cold-storage accounts  
- People who want effortless rewards

## How It Works
1. You install the hook on your account.  
2. You choose the time of your first claim.  
3. The network triggers the schedule automatically.  
4. Every month, the hook wakes up and claims your reward.

That’s it — fully on-chain, no servers, no third parties.

## Safe & Transparent
- Only performs a ClaimReward transaction  
- Cannot move funds or modify your account  
- Hook code and behavior are fully visible and verifiable
